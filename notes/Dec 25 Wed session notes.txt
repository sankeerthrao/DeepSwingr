### 2. Codebase Exploration
Explored and explained the full architecture:
- main.py: Interactive CLI for trajectory/optimization
- tesseracts/: 5 containerized physics modules
  - simplephysics: Empirical physics with neural network
  - jaxphysics: JAX-CFD based Navier-Stokes solver
  - integrator: Diffrax ODE solver for trajectory
  - swing: Swing calculation module
  - optimiser: Brent's method optimization
- plotter/: 3D Plotly visualization
- helper/docker.py: Container orchestration

### 3. Training

- simplephysics: Best val loss = 0.000905 (100 epochs)
Weights saved to tesseracts/*/weights/*.msgpack
